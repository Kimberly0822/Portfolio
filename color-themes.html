My Color Themes
Name Pictures Explanation
Moonlight This theme took inspiration from the monochromatic color scheme. All the colors are very similar so you get that monotone feeling from it. But with the last three being lighter you still get a nice pop from the dark blue. I think this theme could be useful for someone who wants a more calming effect to their website or a site advertising night time activities like camping or a drive-in theater
Beach Sunset For the Beach Sunset theme I tried to use the complementary scheme though it might be considered a bit of spilt as well. The blue and orange are opposite each other so should work well with making the other color pop. The pale yellow I added to resemble the sun and sand and to expand the color theme a bit more. Just from the name clearly these colors might work well for a website promoting tropical vacations
Cherry Blossom I partly made this theme because I love cherry blossoms and to show off the analogous scheme. Although it could be considered a bit monochromatic as well. While the pinks aren't on a basic color wheel the fact they are all near each other on a complex wheel makes it analogous. For this theme I think someone who wants a more feminine look or maybe someone who sells flowers could like all the pinks for their site
Halloween The Halloween theme took slight influence from the
split scheme. While not fully accurate because the
purple isn't being used as the base, it still uses
the orange and yellow that are to the side of the
color opposite it. Obviously this theme would be
great for a website that's Halloween related. Like
one selling costumes or pumpkins or even promoting
haunted houses
Pastel Spring For this theme I tried to make one that kinda used
the triadic scheme. Because of the pastel approach
I took, I had to just use a tint of red which is why
one is pink and the white I added to expand the palette
some. The pastel look makes me think this theme might
work well with a website that caters to babies or
younger kids so like a daycare service or elementary
school
